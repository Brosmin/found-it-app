Welcome to our FOUND IT Information System

Our mission is to help reunite people with their lost belongings and provide a platform for managing found items across campus. We understand how frustrating it can be to lose something important, and we're here to help.

How it works:
  • Found an item? Post it on our platform with details and location where it was found.
  • Lost something? Browse through our database of found items to see if your item has been turned in.
  • Contact information: Each post includes contact details so you can reach out directly.
  • Categories: Items are organized by categories to make searching easier.
Our commitment:
  • All items are reviewed by our staff before being published
  • We maintain accurate location information for better tracking
  • Your privacy and security are our top priorities
  • We work to ensure quick responses to inquiries
Remember: Every item posted brings us one step closer to reuniting someone with their lost belongings. Your contribution matters!
